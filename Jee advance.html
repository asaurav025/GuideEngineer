JEE Advance

IIT are best government engineering colleges in India. It is very difficult to qualify for any of the IIT because the levels of questons are very high. The format of the paper is uncertain and it keeps on changing every year. There's no sample paper published to guess about the format of paper because of all these factors, it's one of the most competitive examinations all over the world.
The examination consists of two papers, paper 1 and paper 2. Duration of each paper is 3 hours followed by 1 hour break in between both the papers. Maximum marks of the paper is also not fixed and it also keeps on changing every year. In 2015 it was of 504 marks, in your turn it may be of any combination of 3 digits.
The question paper of JEE advance is made by any of the top 5 or 6 IIT.
