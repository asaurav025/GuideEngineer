COMEDK

This is an private association which serves students according to their merit to get admitted in different colleges of Karnataka. ComedK stands for Constrium of Medical, Engineering and Dental colleges of Karnataka. This entrance examination provide a portal for admission in all the colleges in Bengaluru and other cities.

List of top colleges which consider comedK marks are mentioned below

  • BMS College of engineering
  • Dayananda College of Engineering
  • MS Ramaiah Institute of Technolgy
  • PES Institute of Technology
  • RV College of Engineering

There are more than 150 colleges in Karnataka including engineering, medical and dental colleges. Competition for top 10 colleges are very high. Application form for entrance examination is available from February and the examination is being held in the month of may.Examination isbeing held in all the major cities of every state.

Exam Pattern

Entrance examination paper consists of 180 multiple choice question and each question carries 1 marks and there is no negetive marks for wrong answer

Section 1- Mathematics 60 question

Section 2- Physics 60 question

Section 3- Chemistry 60 question

Fee structure and Counselling procedure

At the time of admission an amount of 50,000 is paid to the respective college to ensure that you will be taking admission in that particular college. As there are more that 150 colleges therefore the fees vary a bit. Maximum fees of any college under comedk does not cross 1,40,000 annualy.

While going for the counselling make sure that you carry your rank card with yourself. Without this you wont be marked present on the counselling day. Candidtae should also carry original documents of secondry examination and bio-metric verification with themselves(mediacal certificate).
