West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination

This examination is conducted by the state government of West Bengal. Appearing and qualifying this examination allow admission to many government, private engineering institutes and colleges in West Bengal. Candidates who have qualified there secondry board are allowed to appear for the examination. Every year arround 3 lakhs people appear for this examination from the nearby states of Jharkhand, Bihar, Orrissa and obviously West Bengal and few others. The paper consists of three subjects Physics Chemistry and Maths and follows the following format.

Subjects Type 1 Type 2 Type 3 Total Questions Maximum Marks
Mathematics 50*1 marks 15*2 marks 10*2 marks 75 100
Physics 30*1 marks 5*2 marks 5*2 marks 40 50
Chemistry 30*1 marks 5*2 marks 5*2 marks 40 50

Type 1 consists of four option out of which only one option is correct. Each corrects answer awards 1 marks and wrong answer award -0.25 marks.

Type 2 consists of four option out of which only one option is correct. Each corrects answer awards 2 marks and wrong answer award -0.50 marks.

Type 3 may or may have single answer correct, more than one answer may be correct. This category does not have any negetive marking. Marks is awarded by 2*(no of correct answer marked) /(total number of correct answer)). For any wrong option marked, no marks will be awarded
