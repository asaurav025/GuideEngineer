OJEE

Odisha Joint Entrance Examination (OJEE) is a state-government controlled centralised test for admission to many private and governmental medical, engineering & management institutions in Odisha. The application form is released in the month of February and continues till March. Exam is being held in the month of May

List of few top colleges taking part

  • Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology, Sambalpur
  • Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology, Sarang
  • Government College of Engineering, Kalahandi
  • Silicon Institute of Technology, Bhubaneswar
  • Central Institute of Plastic Engineering and Technology, Bhubaneswar
  • Orissa School of Mining Engineering, Keonjhar
  • CV Raman College of Engineering, Bhubaneswar
  • Parala Maharaja Engineering College, Berhampur
  • Gandhi Institute of Engineering and Technology, Gunupur
  • Gandhi Institute for Technological Advancement, Bhubaneswar

Examination Pattern

  • Subjects from which questions would be asked in the exam will be Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics.
  • All the three sections of subjects will weigh equally.
  • For any un-attempted question, no marks will be deducted.
  • The paper I will be held in offline and online mode but Paper-II will only be held in offline mode.
  • Type of questions to be asked in the exam would be of multiple choice type.
  • Candidates have to mark one answer from the given set of 4 options.
  • There would be negative marking for marking incorrect answers. For each wrong answer, ¼ marks shall be deducted.

Counselling Process

Documents required at the time of counselling

  • Class X, XII Mark Sheet and pass certificate
    • School Leaving Certificate
  • JEE Main 2016 Rank Card
  • Green Card issued by Family Welfare Department of Odisha
  • Certificate in support of NRI
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Class X certificate showing the date of birth
  • Caste certificate
