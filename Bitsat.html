Birla Institute of Technology

Admission to this University undergoes certain process. You need to first qualify online BITSAT examination. This university have branches in Goa, Pilani and Hyderabad out of which Pilani is the best. Eligibility for appearing in BITSAT exams are candidates should have passed his/her secondary examination from registered board. Candidates must secure an aggregate of 75% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Maths with at least 60% marks in each subject. Admission will be made according to the merit basis, the marks scored in BITSAT examination. Direct admission is provided to all the board toppers of central as well as state boards.

Each question consists of four option and reward you 3 marks for each correct answer and -1 for each wrong answer. No marks are awarded if answer is unmarked. The paper consists of 4 parts

Part1- Physics paper of 40 questions.

Part2- Chemistry paper of 40 questions.

Part3- English of 15 questions and Logical reasoning of 10 questions respectively.

Part4- Mathematics or Biology paper of 40 questions.

As soon as the entire 150 question is answered, extra 12 questions are given which consists of 4 questions each from Physics Chemistry and Math. And duration of 3 hours is given to solve the whole paper. BITSAT follow the syllabus of NCERT of standard 11 and 12. Bitsat examination is held in 51 big cities. It is not that tough to score marks but it's tough to cross the cut off. To remain in the safe side you should try to bring more than 300 out of 450.The interesting part of this examination is that as soon as you click on submit button your marks will be displayed at your screen.
