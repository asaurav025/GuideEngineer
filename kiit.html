KIIT University

KIIT University, formerly Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, is a co-educational, autonomous university located at Bhubaneswar in the Indian state of Odisha. It was established in 1992 and is well known for its good placement, better environment and great campus. Application form is available from the month of December and goes till March. Admit card is released in the month of april and online entrance exam is also conducted in the last week of April. There are about 120 centres all over India.

Exam Pattern

Duration of examination is 180 minutes.The paper consists of three section and contains total 120 question

Physics 40 questions

Chemistry 40 questions

Mathematics 40 questions

For each correct answer candidates are awarded 4 marks and 1 marks is deducted for wrong answer. Candidates appearing online should be very careful because the timer starts automaticaly and the paper is submitted as soon as the time gets over

Fee Structure and Counselling process

Course Fee Per Semester - Rs 55,000.
Health Insurance - Rs 30,000(It is one time Payable).
Accidental Insurance - Rs 50,000(One Time Payable).
Registration Fee Per Exam - Rs 1000.
Examination Fee - Rs. 1000 Annually.
Total Course Fee - Rs. 768000

Important Information :

  • Download the Rank Card
  • Candidates have to report at the counselling venue half an hour before the time of counselling.
  • Candidates should check the KIIT website to get updated information regarding counselling.
  • Counselling will be stopped at any time as soon as all the seats are filled up.

Candidates should produce following Original Certificates with one set of Xerox Copies on the day of counselling. Original Certificates will be returned after verification and Xerox Copies will be retained by the university for office use.
1. KIITEE-2016 Admit Card
2. KIITEE-2016 Rank Card
3. 10th Pass Certificate / Marksheet
4 . 12th Marksheet (For Pass out candidates)
5. 12th Admit Card (For Appearing Candidates)
6. Two numbers of passport size color photographs
7. Demand Draft of Rs. 75,000/- which includes the counselling registration fees of Rs. 10,000/- in favour of KIIT, payable at Bhubaneswar. Cash payment will not be accepted. Balance fees is to be deposited by 10th June,2016.
8. Reserved category
(i) SC/SC(W)/ST/ST(W) - Relevant Certificate issued by the Competent Authority, clearly indicating the Reservation Criteria claimed by the candidate.
(ii) PC/PC(W) - Physically Challenged certificate issued by authorized Medical Board.
