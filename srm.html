SRM University

The SRM University (Sri Ramaswamy Memorial University), earlier known as SRM Institute of Science and Technology, is a deemed university in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It was established in 1985 as SRM Engineering College in Kattankulathur, under University of Madras. It has 7 campuses out of which 4 are in Tamil Nadu.
The university has been ranked as India's No. 1 private university by Zee News, Deccan Chronicle and India Today. The university has managed 8th position according to Careers 360 as Top Private Universities in India 2015. Application form for applying in this university is available till the month of march and then then slot booking procedure takes place. Computer based examination get started from second half of April.

Exam Pattern

Computer based entrance examination is being held in different cities. Each question has four option out of which only one option is correct. 1 marks is deducted for every wrong answer and three marks is awarded for every correct answer

Part 1: Pysics paper of 35 question

Part 2: Chemistry paper of 35 question

Part 3: Mathematics paper of 35 question

Total marks: 315

Fee structure and counselling process

Annual academic fee charged by SRM university is 1,50,000 excluding hostel fee. Hostel and mess would charge around 1,00,000 per year. Overall its quite expensive but loan is easily available for these colleges.

After getting the call letter you need to carry all your documents to any counselling centre. Documents includes-

  • SRMJEE counselling call letter
  • SRMJEE rak card
  • SRMJEE hall ticket
  • Class 10 mark sheet
  • Class 12/Equivalent mark sheet
  • Demand Draft of specified amount
  • Documents related to casts(if other than general)
