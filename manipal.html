Manipal University

Manipal Academy of Higher Education, branded as Manipal University is a deemed university located in Manipal, Karnataka, India. It has over 28,000 students from 52 countries. The university has branch campuses in Bangalore, Mangalore, Sikkim, Jaipur, Dubai, Malaysia and Antigua. It is a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities. The main campus cover an area of 600 acres land. Application form is available from the month of march and the exam is conducted online in all the major cities of India. Application fee is Rs 600 and entrance examination fees is Rs 1400.

Examination Pattern

Duration of the examination is 150 mins and candidates are offered 200 multiple choice questions. The questions are divided as show below

Physics-50 questions

Chemistry-50 questions

Mathematics-70 questions

English and General Aptitude-30 questions

Each question carries 4 marks for correct answer, -1 marks for wrong answer and null for not attempting. Therefore the exam is conducted of 800 marks and admission is based fully upon the merit basis

Fee structure and Counselling procedure

1st year including admission fees- Rs 2,70,000

2nd year- Rs 3,39,000

3rd year- Rs 3,38,000

4th year- Rs 3,38,000

Total course fees- Rs 12,85,000

As you book yourself for the online test, remember also to select your venue for counselling from the list of centres available in the online test booking system (OTBS), before you download the e-hall ticket. All the original documents need to be carried like admit card, application form, secondry examination result along with medical certificate and identity proof.
