Vellore Institute of technology

Vit is a private university which is recently at its height in every field whther in acedemics, placement, campus etc. Earlier private colleges was assumed to be equivlent to bad colleges. But now the thinking is changed after vit managed to acquire position among top 20 colleges in MHRD lists. Candidates are also very excited about this college because of very well mantained and luxurious campus. Because of its briliant performance again one important factor which emerges out is competition. There's an advantage for general cast as their is no reservation for SC/ST or other lower casts.

Admission in VIT University is conducted on a merit basis through an online entrance exam. VIT organises its own entrance exam, called the Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination (VITEEE). It is conducted every year in the month of April and May. In 2015 over two lakh students appeared for the entrance examination. Admissions to postgraduate courses is done through the separate VITMEE test held in the first week of June every year.
